3 Apartment Looks You'll Want To Recreate ASAP

August 26, 2022 4 Min read

Table of Contents

Warm Organic Neo-Traditional Moody Moment

After years of dorm life, it can be overwhelming to move into your first real apartment. Do not let those bare walls and furniture-less rooms get you down! If you are wondering how you should style your apartment, we are here to help with small apartment ideas and 1 bedroom apartment design ideas galore. To take the guesswork out of styling your new space, we have tapped Dormify experts to reveal the apartment looks they are loving right now.


Warm Organic

Minimalism gets warmer with this look, which combines clean lines and modern shapes with muted, earthy colors, natural textures like wood, and plenty of plants. It is modern and stylish, with just enough of an edge to keep things interesting and comfortable.

Neo-Traditional

Step into the good life in this neo-traditional space that mixes traditional silhouettes with modern accents. The pop of subtle blue hues paired with the warmth of neutral furniture creates the perfect space to kick back and relax.

Moody Moment

From forest green to deep slate gray, we can not get enough of sophisticated dark hues. Create the cozy and moody look in your space by incorporating rich hues like charcoal, navy, black, hunter green or aubergine, layered textures, and mixed metal accents.
