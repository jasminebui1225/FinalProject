Pantry Door Ideas To Maximize Your Kitchens Potential

May 30, 2022 5 Min read

Table of Contents

Tips Our Suggestion

Pantry door ideas are not at the top of the kitchen renovation list. However, kitchen pantries receive attention because they are practical. The doors protect the inside space that holds your dry goods and perishables while providing an element of style for your kitchen space.


Tips

A pantry door helps with your kitchen organizing system. With the amount of time you spend in your kitchen, you want the best pantry style for your home. Here, we will show you pantry door ideas to inspire your next kitchen renovation. Pantry doors and pocket doors are functional. The doors give your home a contemporary feel. If you are spending time thinking about installing a new pantry door, determine which direction it should swing open. Next, what do you want it to look like, and see if the door is necessary.

Pantry Storage Items

This is useful if you are planning to build a new pantry or if you want to redesign an existing one. It helps you determine what types of interior storage you should prioritize based on your habits and needs.



Location

If you already have a pantry, examine whether it has the best placement. You might discover that your current pantry is in an inconvenient spot.


Size

A walk-in pantry does not have to be big to be practical. You need enough room between storage areas to move around freely. If possible, include a walkway between 42in and 48in wide so two people can enter at the same time. Pantry shelves should not be too deep so that you can reach things at the very back. A depth of 14 inches is plenty and 16 inches is the maximum. The space between pantry shelves is often wasted, so leave room for large items.


Lighting

A poorly-lit pantry interior makes it hard to find things. Also, it is easier to keep a well-lit pantry clean and organized.

Suggestion 1: Frosted Glass Door

A pantry door with glass is one thing, but one with frosted glass is another. With a frosted glass pantry door, the kitchen has visual interest when you choose a glass pantry door with frosted glass. This glass style lets you see inside but blurs the details and hides the contents inside the pantry. Check out thriftydecorchick for more inspiration.

Suggestion 2: Space-Saving Sliding Door

Sliding barn doors are gorgeous and well-suited as pantry doors. They help save space and are easy to build. The forward sliding mechanism is a nice kitchen element. Paint the door a fun color to add character to your home.

Suggestion 3: Pantry Door Spice Rack

Attaching shelves inside your pantry door creates more storage space. You can build a small unit with shallow shelves deep enough to hold your spices, for example. Details for this project are on shanty-2-chic.

Suggestion 4: Include Blackboard

Make pantry doors stand out in your kitchen with eye-catching and practical painted blackboards. They are handy for writing notes to family members or making a grocery list.
